What You Get
Inside Every
Cold Start Pack
01
Re-Entry Sequence
Exactly which files to read first, ranked by importance score. Rebuild 80% of your mental model in 30 minutes.
02
File Churn Heatmap
What changes most, visualized. High churn means high importance OR high instability. Know before you touch.
03
Implicit Coupling
Files that always change together — the hidden architecture your diagram never shows. The source of 80% of bugs.
04
Danger Zones
AI-identified areas where changes cascade silently. Know what to test before you break something in production.
05
Commit DNA
Every commit classified by intent: feature, fix, refactor, perf, docs. See the team culture at a glance.
06
Key Decisions
AI extracts why the code is shaped the way it is. The architectural choices buried in commit history, surfaced.
